// About me

Engineering autonomous systems for a smarter world.

I'm a Full Stack Robotics Engineer who works across hardware, software and signals — writing the firmware, tuning the controllers, designing the RF front-end and shipping the software that ties everything together.

My work spans autonomous drones, mobile robots (AMRs), six-axis industrial arms, and the RF & waveguide systems that let machines sense and communicate. I care about systems that don't just work in a demo — they hold up in the real world. I enjoy taking a problem from a blank workbench to a robot that reliably does its job.

What I build.

Six domains, one end-to-end stack. Tap a domain to filter the projects.

  • 01 Autonomous Drones Flight stacks, CFD-tuned airframes & vision-guided delivery.
  • 02 AMR & Mobile Robots LiDAR SLAM, ROS/ROS2 nav stacks & live dashboards.
  • 03 Robot Manipulators Custom inverse kinematics & vision-guided pick-and-place.
  • 04 RF & Waveguide mmWave sensing, pyramidal waveguide design & beam shaping.
  • 05 AI & Computer Vision Deep learning, object detection & real-time perception.
  • 06 Industrial & Hardware Fabrication, controls, IoT & end-to-end hardware.

One engineer. The whole pipeline.

Most robots pass through a dozen hands. Mine go from raw material to autonomous behaviour through one — every stage below is work I do myself.

🛠️

Design & Fabricate

CAD, gears & transmissions, sheet metal, welding, CNC and 3D printing — the physical machine, built end to end.

⚡

Electronics & Embedded

Custom boards, ESP/STM firmware, sensor–driver–encoder integration and control-panel wiring over UART, I²C, SPI, CAN & RS-485.

👁️

Perception & Vision

OpenCV, YOLO and Roboflow with sensor fusion — face recognition, object detection and real-world dimensioning.

🧭

Autonomy & Motion

ROS/ROS2, SLAM, navigation, forward & inverse kinematics and motion control that make the machine act on its own.
